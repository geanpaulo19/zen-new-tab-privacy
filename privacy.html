Zen New Tab - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Zen New Tab is a minimal Chrome extension providing time, date, daily mantras, and beautiful background images. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

2. Data Collection

This extension does not collect any personal data from you. It operates entirely locally in your browser.

3. API Usage

The extension fetches data from the following sources:

  • Unsplash (via Vercel proxy): Retrieves daily background images without exposing API keys.
  • Pexels (via Vercel proxy): Retrieves background images.
  • Lorem Picsum: Retrieves random background images.
  • ZenQuotes & TypeFit: Retrieves daily mantras and quotes.

No personal information is sent to these APIs. All requests are anonymous.

4. Local Storage

The extension stores minimal temporary data locally in your browser:

  • Cached background image (to reduce API calls)

All stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

5. Third-Party Links

Background image credits may link to Unsplash or Pexels photographer pages. Please see their respective Unsplash privacy policy and Pexels privacy policy for more information.

6. No Tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising. All extension features operate locally on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If you update the extension, please review this page to stay informed.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please contact the developer at geanpaulo@mail.com.
