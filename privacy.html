Zen Tabs - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Zen Tabs is a minimal Chrome extension providing time, date, daily mantras, and beautiful background images. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

2. Information We Collect

Zen Tabs does not collect any personal information from you. The extension only stores:

  • Your preferred time format (12-hour or 24-hour)
  • Cached background image for faster loading

This data is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local.

3. Background Images

Background images are fetched from Unsplash each day. No additional user data is sent to Unsplash beyond the request to retrieve an image.

4. Third-Party Services

The only external service used is Unsplash for background images. Please see their privacy policy for more information.

5. No Tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising. All extension features operate locally on your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If you update the extension, please review this page to stay informed.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact the developer at geanpaulo@mail.com.
